"Lead like a girl" Fellowship | Shenomics

July 2018 - Dec 2018

I am proud to be a certified fellow for the "Lead like a girl" fellowship offered by Shenomics. Out of the 20 fellows selected throughout India, I got training around mindfulness, mindful leadership and how to lead from within. This six-month journey from July'18 to Dec'18 guidance of experienced mentors and coaches helped me channelize my strengths towards causes I really care for. In order to create to a ripple effect by impacting 100 other girls, I have started my own social initiative called "Girls and Keys" and it's currently in it's alpha phase.
